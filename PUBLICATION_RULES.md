# Publication Rules

This repository publishes public educational knowledge for Rakos Media Group.

The purpose is to help people, search engines, AI search systems, answer engines, and future agents understand Rakos Media Group, its services, concepts, frameworks, and areas of expertise.

This repository should publish the what and the why.

It should not publish the full private how.

## Publish

The following content is allowed in this repository:

- Definitions
- Concepts
- Framework overviews
- FAQs
- Thought leadership
- Industry education
- High-level strategy
- Public service descriptions
- Public entity descriptions
- Public methodology summaries
- General best practices
- Public-facing examples

## Protect

The following content should not be published in this repository:

- Private prompts
- Internal SOPs
- Client strategies
- Client data
- Audit rubrics
- Scoring systems
- Automation logic
- API keys
- Passwords
- Private workflows
- Internal decision trees
- Internal research process
- Proprietary implementation details
- Unpublished client research
- Sensitive business information

## Guiding Principle

Publish the what and the why.

Protect the exact how.

## Safe Content Examples

Safe:

- What entity SEO means
- Why structured data matters
- What AI search optimization is
- How knowledge graphs support visibility at a high level
- Why websites need clear service pages
- Why Google Ads campaigns need conversion tracking
- What makes a business easier for search engines and AI systems to understand

Not safe:

- The exact internal audit checklist
- Private scoring models
- Full client strategy documents
- Private Codex prompts
- API credentials
- Client campaign data
- Internal automation workflows
- Private decision trees

## Review Before Publishing

Before publishing a file, check:

1. Does this reveal a private prompt?
2. Does this reveal a scoring model?
3. Does this expose a client strategy?
4. Does this include client data?
5. Does this reveal an internal workflow?
6. Does this include credentials or tokens?
7. Does this give away a proprietary process in a way that can be copied directly?

If the answer is yes, do not publish it in this repository.

Move it to the private knowledge repository instead.
