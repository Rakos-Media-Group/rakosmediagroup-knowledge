## Why This Exists

Search is changing.

Traditional SEO focused heavily on keywords, links, and pages. Modern search systems also need to understand entities, relationships, context, services, authority signals, and structured information.

AI systems do not just look for pages. They retrieve, summarize, compare, recommend, and cite information.

That means businesses need more than content. They need clear digital infrastructure that explains who they are, what they do, who they serve, what they know, and why they are credible.

Rakos Media Group uses this repository to organize that knowledge in a clear, structured, and machine-readable way.
